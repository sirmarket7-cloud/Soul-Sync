AI Training

Your conversations may be used anonymously to train MyTwin models. We do not share your personal data. You can opt out from Settings.

1. Data Collection 🔒

We only collect data necessary to operate the service: email, conversations, and preferences. We do not collect sensitive data without your explicit consent.

2. Data Usage 👁️

We use your data to provide personalized service and improve your experience. Conversations are used anonymously for AI training.

3. Data Storage ☁️

All your data is encrypted and stored on secure Supabase servers. You own your data and can request deletion anytime.

4. Data Sharing 🛡️

We do not share your personal data with third parties. We never sell your data.

5. Your Rights 📋

You can access, modify, export, or delete your data anytime from Settings.

6. Minors Privacy 👶

The app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are a parent and believe your child used the app, please contact us to delete their data immediately.

7. Third-Party Services 🔗

MyTwin uses external services including: Supabase (database), Railway (hosting), OpenRouter/Groq/Gemini (AI models), OneSignal (notifications), Edge TTS/ElevenLabs (text-to-speech), PostHog (analytics).

8. Contact Us ✉️

For any privacy questions or data requests, email us at: privacy@soulsync.com. We will respond within 48 hours.

Medical & Psychological Disclaimer: MyTwin is a companion AI app for entertainment and supportive purposes only. It is not a doctor or therapist. If you are in crisis, use the 'Emergency Mental Support' feature or contact professional services.

AI Notice: AI-generated responses may be inaccurate, inappropriate, or contain errors (hallucinations). Use the app consciously and do not rely on twin responses as your sole source of information.
