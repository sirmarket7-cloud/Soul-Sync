Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Your Data Belongs to You 🔒

We only collect data necessary to operate the app: email, name (optional), twin preferences, and chat history. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising without your explicit consent.

2. Where is Your Data Stored? ☁️

Your data is stored on secure Supabase cloud servers. We use standard security measures (TLS, AES-256) to protect your data, but we do not implement end-to-end encryption (E2EE). Data access is restricted to authorized systems and services necessary for platform operation only.

3. How Does AI Processing Work? 🧠

MyTwin uses multiple AI models (Gemini, Groq, OpenRouter). Some messages may be processed on these models' servers according to their privacy policies. We do not control how these models process data, and we recommend reviewing their policies.

4. Data Retention & Deletion 🗑️

We retain data throughout your use of the app. You can request account deletion and data removal anytime via Settings > Delete Account. Data may be automatically deleted after a period of inactivity (based on your plan).

5. Export Your Data 📤

You have the right to export all your data in JSON format. You can do this from Settings > Export My Data. We will send you the file within 48 hours.

Medical & Psychological Disclaimer: MyTwin is a companion AI app for entertainment and supportive purposes only. It is not a doctor or therapist. If you are in crisis, use the 'Emergency Mental Support' feature or contact professional services.

AI Notice: AI-generated responses may be inaccurate, inappropriate, or contain errors (hallucinations). Use the app consciously and do not rely on twin responses as your sole source of information.

6. Minors Privacy 👶

The app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are a parent and believe your child used the app, please contact us to delete their data immediately.

7. Third-Party Services 🔗

MyTwin uses external services including: Supabase (database), Railway (hosting), OpenRouter/Groq/Gemini (AI models), OneSignal (notifications), Edge TTS/ElevenLabs (text-to-speech), PostHog (analytics).

8. Contact Us ✉️

For any privacy questions or data requests, email us at: support@mytwin.app. We will respond within 48 hours.
