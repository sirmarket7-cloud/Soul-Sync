Help Center

How Can We Help?

Answers to frequently asked questions and ways to contact us

📱

How do I start?

Download the app from Google Play, create an account, and start a conversation with your twin. Personalization takes just a few minutes.

💳

How do I subscribe?

Go to Settings > Plans and choose your preferred plan. We accept credit cards and Google Pay.

🗑️

How do I delete my account?

Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

📤

How do I export my data?

Settings > Privacy > Export My Data. You will receive the JSON file within 48 hours.

🔒

Are my chats secure?

We use TLS and AES-256 encryption. However, we do not offer end-to-end encryption. See our Privacy Policy.

⚕️

Is MyTwin a doctor?

No. MyTwin is a companion AI for entertainment and supportive purposes only. Consciousness is simulated — not real awareness. Not a substitute for medical care.

🎙️

How do I use voice?

Tap the microphone icon in the chat. Supports 19 Arabic dialects + English.

🌙

What is proactive awareness?

Your twin sends automatic messages: reminders, support, congratulations. You can adjust the frequency in settings.

Didn't find your answer?

Our support team is ready to help. We respond within 48 hours.
